Group Reffection

At the end of this project, as we are reflecting on our journey, there are many things that can be improved, but all in all, this project brought us precious experiences and great time working as a team. Firstly, we have not finished the whole project, which we all feel a little down in the dumps. Although we planned it well, brainstormed on the idea well, as we went to execute, we realized there were a lot we had yet to know about IT world. Not so surprising it is coined as a ‘world’. But from that surprise, we had the chance to proactively look for solutions, looking for short-term online courses about web development, spending extra time to dive deeper into what we had learned in class. And if we can turn back time, we would spend more time together to work on this project face to face, since we felt that working this way would bring us more energy and motivation. That was also one mistake we made when we mostly met online and only faced each other once a week. We were surprised at how hard developing a game website could be, and the depth of making an engaging website was even harder. It took a lot of small details to create a big and beautiful picture as the picture created in our idea. We have learned that working in a group, specifically in an IT project, we need patience and lots of hard work. That was more valuable than finishing this project since we are freshman, we see the value of learning and practicing can make perfect, we see things that we can bring with us to the workplace, to our daily lives, values that we could keep and improve from for a lifetime. Another mistake we made was not strictly set small deadlines for the details, that brought us to a small level of frustration at the near end of the project when we had to chase the clock to wrap things up. But that was also when we learned how to keep our head cool, focused and tried our best to finish the web. We would say this project was a meaningful experience for us to get to know each other, getting to know the tendency of how the workplace will be and knowing how to work under pressure, knowing how to self-learn to overcome our own limitation. We had learned invaluable practical skills on web development, using technology software, communicating with our colleagues, and the most valuable thing we learn was that under a fresh, creative, comfortable environment of working, we can thrive and perform at our best.
