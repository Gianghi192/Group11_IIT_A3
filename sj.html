Skills and Jobs

Project Manager

Responsibilities

- Set the goals and manage the project plan including timeline.
- Assign tasks and organize the entire project.
- Keep an open line of communication with team members.
- Ensure the project serves its functionality and on-time completion.

Skills Required

- Leader skills.
- Organization and communication skills.

Technical Expert

Responsibilities

- Implement user interface and interactive, visual and audio elements.
- Create server side and database management system.
- Ensure the website runs smoothly on a variety of browsers and devices.
- Ensure the security and the integrity of the data.

Skills Required

- Problem-solving and logical thinking skills.
- Expertise with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
- Knowledge of both front-end (HTML5) and back-end (Node.js) framework.
- Proficiency in database management system including SQL and NoSQL.
- Familiarity with GitHub repository.
- Knowledge of DevOps for continuous integration and continuous deployment (CD/CI).

Content Developer

Responsibilities

- Design the storytelling and puzzle theme of the room game.
- Maintain the user-friendly interface and easy access.
- Optimize the audio assets including sound effects and background music.
- Take responsibility for all the visual elements

Skills Required

- Imaginative skills and creativity.
- Expert in graphic design software: Adobe, Photoshop, Illustrator.
- Proficiency in sound editing software: Audacity, Adobe Audition.

Quality Assurance (QA) Tester

Responsibilities

- Collaborate with technical team.
- Define and execute testing to identify bugs then fix it.
- Ensure the web runs without any bugs

Skills Required

- Problem-solving skills.
- Methodologies for testing.

Market Research Analyst

Responsibilities

- Research the market trend and existing escape room website competitors.
- Gather and analyze the market trends.
- Get insight into the project to meet the target audience.
- Conduct survey and analyze feedback from users to develop website.

Skills Required

- Research and analyze skills.
- Logical thinking and problem-solving skills.
- Communication and time management.
